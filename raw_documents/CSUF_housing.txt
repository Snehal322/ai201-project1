Planning to live on campus next academic year? The Cal State Fullerton Housing Application is now open. Students seeking on-campus housing for the fall and spring semesters are required to complete the online application through the Housing Portal.

  
Information about the Housing Application is presented across several pages. Begin by reviewing the application overview on this page, then proceed to the pages below for details on eligibility requirements, application steps, and what to expect prior to applying for on-campus housing.

 

Please note that this year, the Housing Application is open for all class levels at the same time, and payment will be accepted when it opens. We strongly encourage you to be online and ready to submit your application with payment on:

 

Monday, February 2nd, 2026 at 10 AM PDT

 

Please note the following:

There is no requirement to live on campus.
Housing is not guaranteed.
Offers are sent are on a first-come, first-served basis.
Priority is given to students who apply early.

Where and How to Apply
Applications are processed on a first-come, first-served basis as space allows.

Housing application is separate from CSUF admission
Students must accept their admission to CSUF before they can see the housing application
Access may take 2-3 business days after admission acceptance and payment of the enrollment administrative charge
Students must apply through the CSUF Housing Portal.

HOUSING PORTAL


Application Fee
The application fee is $50
The fee is mandatory and non-refundable
Payment must be made using a debit or credit card
Checks and bank transfers are not accepted

After Applying
Housing offers are sent on a rolling basis
Students must check their CSUF email daily
Missing an email is not an acceptable reason for missed deadlines
Offers are only emailed to students
No parents, supporters, or guarantors receive housing emails
 

Carefully review each section of the housing application before submitting. By completing and submitting your application, you agree to the terms and conditions outlined in the License Agreement, which constitutes a legally binding agreement between you and Housing and Residential Engagement.

 

All official communication from Housing and Residential Engagement will be sent to your Cal State Fullerton student email address. Please check your CSUF email regularly, as many messages contain important and time-sensitive information that may require a prompt response. Housing is not responsible for missed communications if you fail to check your student email account.

FAQ
Financial Aid Awards
If you have an anticipated financial aid award, any remaining funds after your tuition and general fees have been paid will be applied toward your housing fees. Your anticipated aid award must be sufficient to cover your tuition and installment amount due in order to avoid the $30 late fee. 

Financial Aid award cannot be applied toward any late fees.
